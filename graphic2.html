KNOWMADIKA BRANDING


Knowmadika is a mobile application designed to facilitate content-rich nomadic inquiry-based learning and engagement for K-12 students in a museum through text, audio, and photo data collection. The client requested for "vivid, bright, and fresh colors".

Based on this information, a logotype style guide and web mockups have been designed.

This project was completed for a graphic design class.
