THE TASKER
INTERACTION DESIGN


The Tasker is a project management mobile application featuring the ability to add members to a task or folder, in-app chat & Google Doc editing, and progress charts to encourage motivation. Several methods involved in the process of this design include:

  • Brainstorming
  • Comparative Analysis
  • 40 Solution Sketches
  • Cultural Probes
  • Storyboarding & Scenarios
  • Lo-fi Paper Prototyping & Testing
  • Hi-fi Digital Prototyping

Next steps for this project are to design Android, tablet, and desktop versions, create a cloud for syncing across platforms, connect the application to CTools for data retrieval.
